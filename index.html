Photos & Past Events

Highlights from our recent activities and achievements

QFC Hunger Busters Food Drive

Every year, we host "Hunger Busters" at the Canyon Park and Bothell QFCs. At these events, we collect donated food to give to the Hopelink Foundation. For our 2024 Hunger Busters, we donated over 2,250 pounds of food!

2,250+ lbs donated Community Impact

Event Photos

Fall Leadership Conference

Every fall, our region hosts the FBLA Fall Leadership Conference. During this conference, our members listen to industry experts, learn from past national winners, gain points for the member leaderboard, and prepare for regionals. We hope to see you attend next year!

North Creek HS Leadership Skills

Easter Food Drive 2025

This Easter season, our FBLA chapter organized a special food drive to support local families during the holiday. Working with community partners and volunteers, we collected essential food items and Easter treats to bring joy and support to families in need throughout our community.
